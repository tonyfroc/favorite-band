Band Members

Metallica conists of vocalist and guitarist James Hetfield, drummer Lars Ulrich, guitarist Kirk Hammett, and bass guitarist Robert Trujillo. Metallica has had very few changes to its band members through the years. Tragically, their first guitarist, Cliff Burton, passed away in a car accident. Following his death Kirk Hammett joined the band. During this time, Jason Newsted was the main bass guitar player. In the early 2000s Robert Trujillo joined the band as the new bass guitarist.
